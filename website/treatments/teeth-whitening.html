Professionally supervised in-clinic teeth whitening and polishing in Kukatpally, Hyderabad - gum protection, controlled application and a shade record so you can see the difference.

A visibly brighter smile, safely

Teeth pick up colour from everyday life - every cup of tea, every coffee, every year that passes. Whitening reverses that, and the difference between a good result and a sore one comes down to who's holding the gel. Unregulated home kits apply whitening agents to unexamined teeth with no gum protection - a common cause of burning gums, patchy results and sensitivity.

In-clinic whitening at Care32 is different: we check your teeth and gums first, shield the soft tissue, and apply professionally supervised whitening gels in controlled cycles. You leave our Kukatpally clinic with a noticeably brighter, radiant smile - and your enamel intact. And because the whole process is supervised by a dentist, anything that needs attention first - a cavity, tender gums - gets spotted before it can become a problem.

What causes staining?

  • Tea, coffee and red wine - the everyday culprits
  • Tobacco in any form - smoking or chewing
  • Age - enamel thins naturally, letting the darker layer beneath show through
  • Certain medicines taken in childhood or long-term
  • Poor cleaning habits that let plaque and surface stains build up

Surface stains respond to a professional cleaning and polish; deeper discolouration is where whitening makes the real difference. At your consultation we'll look at where your staining sits and tell you honestly which one you need - sometimes the answer is simply a thorough clean.

Your whitening visit - step by step

01

Consultation & shade record

We check that your teeth and gums are healthy, discuss what's realistic for your smile, and record your starting shade.

02

Professional cleaning & polishing

Plaque, tartar and surface stains are removed first, so the whitening gel works evenly on clean enamel.

03

Gum protection

A protective barrier is placed over your gums and lips - the step home kits skip, and the reason in-clinic whitening stays comfortable.

04

Whitening application

Professionally supervised whitening gel is applied to your teeth in controlled cycles, monitored throughout by your dentist.

05

Shade comparison & aftercare advice

We compare your new shade with the starting record so you see the change, then walk you through simple aftercare to keep it bright.

Healthy gums make every smile brighter, too - if yours bleed or feel tender, start with our gum care and professional cleaning first.

Keeping it bright - aftercare

  • Avoid strongly staining food and drink - tea, coffee, wine, turmeric-heavy curries - for the first 48 hours
  • Use a straw for cold staining drinks to keep them off your front teeth
  • Brush twice daily and keep up regular professional polishing visits
  • Skip tobacco - it undoes whitening faster than anything else
  • Book an occasional top-up session to refresh the shade when needed
