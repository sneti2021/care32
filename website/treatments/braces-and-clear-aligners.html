Orthodontics · Ceramic braces · Clear aligners

Braces and clear aligners in Kukatpally, for every age.

From tooth-coloured ceramic braces to nearly invisible clear aligners - orthodontic treatment in Kukatpally, planned digitally so you see where your smile is headed before it starts moving.

Straight teeth, your way

Orthodontics has changed. You no longer have to choose between crooked teeth and two years of conspicuous metal. At Care32, orthodontics is one of our core focus areas - backed by 30+ years of combined specialist experience - and we offer three discreet ways to straighten your smile: ceramic (tooth-coloured) braces, self-ligating clear braces, and removable clear aligners made from BPA-free plastics.

Every case begins with a 3D scan and a digital smile preview, so you can see a realistic picture of your future smile - and understand the plan - before a single bracket or tray is made. Whether you're a student, a working professional or a parent who finally wants to fix what's bothered you for years, there's an option here that fits your life in Hyderabad.

Who is this for?

  • Crowded or overlapping teeth that are hard to clean and easy to hide in photos
  • Gaps between teeth you'd like to close
  • Protruding front teeth that affect your profile or confidence
  • Bite issues - teeth that don't meet comfortably when you chew
  • Relapse after old braces, when teeth have drifted back over the years

If any of these sound like you, a short consultation will tell you exactly what's possible - and which option suits your teeth best.

Your options at Care32

  • Ceramic braces - tooth-coloured brackets that blend with your smile, giving you the reliability of fixed braces without the flash of metal
  • Self-ligating clear braces - a modern bracket design that holds the wire without elastic ties, working with gentle continuous force and often needing fewer adjustment visits
  • Clear aligners - a series of transparent, removable trays made from BPA-free plastic, custom-made from your 3D scan and changed at home as your teeth move

Braces vs. clear aligners

FactorBracesClear Aligners

Aligners only work while they're on your teeth - they need committed daily wear. Our orthodontist will tell you honestly which option your case truly needs.

The journey - step by step

01

3D scan & digital preview

A comfortable digital scan replaces messy impressions. Smile-preview software then shows you a realistic projection of your result before anything begins.

02

Your custom plan

Our orthodontist maps every tooth movement and walks you through the options, timeline and what to expect - in plain language, with a clear estimate.

03

Fitting or first trays

Braces are bonded in a single comfortable appointment, or you receive your first set of custom aligner trays with simple wear instructions.

04

Periodic reviews

Short visits to adjust wires or check tray progress keep everything on track. We watch the details so you just watch your smile change.

05

Retention

Once your teeth are where they belong, retainers hold them there. We show you exactly how to keep your new smile for good.
