What we offer little smiles

  • Gentle check-ups - relaxed, age-appropriate examinations that build trust visit by visit
  • Fissure sealants - a protective coating over the grooves of back teeth where cavities love to start
  • Fluoride treatments - strengthening young enamel against decay
  • Gentle cavity repair - including silver diamine fluoride and laser dentistry for minimally invasive care
  • Pediatric crowns & pulp therapy - saving badly decayed baby teeth so they can do their job until the adult teeth arrive

  • Early orthodontic assessment & jaw growth evaluation - spotting alignment issues while they're easiest to guide
  • Custom mouthguards - protecting sporty smiles on the field
  • Habit guidance - kind, practical help with thumb-sucking and pacifier habits
  • Emergency care - for knocked, chipped or aching teeth, when you need us most

Prevention first

The best dental treatment for a child is the one they never need. Our pediatric care is built around stopping problems before they start:

  • Sealants shield the chewing surfaces of new molars, where most childhood cavities begin
  • Fluoride applications harden enamel during the years it's most vulnerable
  • Habit counselling addresses thumb-sucking and pacifier use before they affect tooth position
  • Diet advice - practical, realistic guidance on snacks, sweets and sipping habits that parents can actually follow

Prevention also means watching development over time. At each visit we keep an eye on how the jaws are growing and how the adult teeth are coming in, so anything that needs guiding gets noticed early - when it's simplest to address.
